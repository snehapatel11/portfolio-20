REPORT ON INTERVENTIONS FROM VISIT TO ABSL

TASK FOR INTERNSHIP SELECTION

Meeting name

Partnership between Unati Multi state cooperative and ABSL

Meeting objective

To develop salt based cooperative economy in Sambar area of Rajasthan

Meeting location

Sambar area, Rajasthan

Meeting host

Hon'ble CMD ABSL Mr. Amitabh Ji

Discussion: We can create a local management committee with ABSL comprising members  from both the parties (ABSL & UNATI).

Action item 1 : Whoever will be appointed by CMD will  create a committee which will be managing all the further planning and possible plans.

Action item 2 : A MOU can be drafted between UAMMCL and ABSL for future working.

Note : MOUs will serve as guidelines, ensuring a win-win situation for both ABSL and UAMMCL. While generating work, simultaneous market development will be pursued by

UAMMCL.

Action item 3 : This Includes creating orders for big B2B and other brands. Exploring better and unique packaging facilities for sambar salt in association with  ABSL.

General idea : (Way forward : There is also a possibility that ABSL can provide space on Rent  for setting up or might be they handover their existing packaging facilities to us  although a new space can be provided and with the help of the local Management  committee will develop the same).

UAMMCL Tasks :

Action Items :

  • UAMMCL will take Ayurvedic and FSSAI  License to operate this unit.
  • UAMMCL will procure algae based technologies and products from ABSL and use it  for fodder in other states like Punjab UP etc for making Animal Feed.

Note : Proposed to transfer these technologies from ABSL platform for scale up to UAMMCL.

Discussion :

  • All the backward integration should be  done by UAMMCL.

note : This will lead to training, capacity building and all the government schemes in our favor.

  • We can use funds initially for backward  integration from govt schemes so that we can make a cooperative structure.
  • UAMMCL  can register internal groups, being an integral part of the co- operative structure  rather than creating new cooperatives.

Note : Once we finalize the packaging facilities then ABSL staff who are working on daily wages will be provided for the membership of UAMMCL and further will be integrated in the same working structure so that all the expenses which were made by ABSL will be eventually undertaken.

 

  • All the operations will be taken care of by UAMMCL.

Note :

  • Capacity building and training will be given to local community people in packing.
  • The production facility can be organically certified.

The extraction process will also  be pursued organically. Mr. Samar Goyal, local from Jaipur, will be the task owner  from Unati's side.

Discussion : Black salt production can also be handed over to UAMMCL with local community  intervention.

Discussion : Packaging can be considered for all existing big clients  with alkaline high value salt.

Note : whereas refined salt packaging will be in our second phase after the commencement and establishment or LMC (local management  committee) will take further decisions (to be mentioned in MOU).

Action item 4 : To combat illegal activities, AI and traceability tools, including satellite mapping, drone surveys, and block chain, will be employed to ensure proper oversight and management.

Note: The objective is to provide traceability of salt along  with GI tag if it can be developed. The current unauthorized entities cannot do this.

Discussion : Local engineering colleges IIT Jodhpur interns can assist in this mapping effort and  also they can be involved in developing low cost machines for salt extraction,  Building block chain for salt collection etc.

Discussion : All government programs like SRLM, (Rural livelihood missions) or even any program present will be explored for building cooperatives of local communities.

Note :

  • Regional  cooperative institute, Jaipur can be helpful.
  • Regional cooperative training institute Jaipur could be a potential  partner, with opportunities for CSR funding integrating SLRM etc.

Action item 5&6

  • All salts will be registered in Ayurveda.
  • Trials will be conducted via Unati Talwara, followed by launching in internal brands like Unati Behan and Asana.

Note : The response  will guide further discussions with major brands.

Discussion :

  • Plans for establishing a packaging facility with ABSL can have two options: renting  of new space or transferring existing facilities on a rent basis. Where UAMMCL can  take facilities on rent for UAMMCL. The reference agreement of Unati and Punjab  State Council can be a reference document.
  • The vision of developing micro-refineries at the community level and operating under cooperative manner was also discussed.
  • Unati Talwara can provide organic Triphala for the same.
  • Technology intervention from IIT Jodhpur or local Engineering college or Mr.  Sudhansu can be taken.
  • Dual Training Programme of technical council GOI Program can be used to train skilled  manpower involving local ITI.
  • The production approach will mirror ABSL's current operations, with UAMMCL acting  as a community interface.
  • Community plantation of Bamboo along the Sambar lake to build bamboo based  communities cooperative.

Note : Bamboo can be a very good income source for communities and ABSL. Salt tolerant bamboo varieties can be introduced in the area.

Discussion :

  • ABSL can give land to cooperative groups for plantations where they can cultivate  bamboo and harvest.
  • The land ownership will be with ABSL and the community will  share revenue with ABSL from each harvest in some ratio.
  • Community based solar farm along the bank of lake. It was discussed to develop a cooperative solar farm along with a bank.
  • ABSL and UAMMCL can jointly establish this  project.

Note : We can Get NABARD funding for this project at a very low interest rate.
